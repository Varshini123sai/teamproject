  • Guest entries can be done only within above timings.
  • The guest entry counter will be closed and no guest coupon can be taken after that.
  • As the caterer may not able to set the full counter in the initial 15 mins.
  • Therefore, we add extra 15 mins buffer (extension) in the end.
  • Therefore, caterer is supposed to serve on the food counter during extended timings
  • After these extended timings, caterer will have to stop serving and take out food from counter.
  • The leftover food may or may not be kept on the counter as the caterer wishes to.
  • No arguments with mess workers will be entertained thereafter.
